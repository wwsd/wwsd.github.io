--- layout: post title: Sacred words date: '2013-02-08T05:58:00.002-08:00' author: Jennifer Rowan tags: modified_time: '2013-02-08T05:58:24.935-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-741389610085892054.post-4157436025939612333 blogger_orig_url: http://splashofamermaid.blogspot.com/2013/02/sacred-words.html ---  I can understand why Hemingway chain smoked and drank like a fish these days.

I"m doing a lot of heavy duty, healing, release writing here lately. 

You know the kind - the words that are hidden the most deeeply within you that are also helping you die the less you let them out.  The kind you wonder if your family may disown you for.  The kind of words that make even you stop mid-porchswing and think - what?  That just took the breath out of me.

This kind of writing requires deep solitude for me.  I can't worry if I'm late for dinner or if I'm affecting someone with my returning thoughts wishing to be written words.  I can't take a break to go to lunch with a gal pal or hit the mall.  My entire being must be focused on the words that need to get out of me.

Thsse words not only heal me, but carry with them the hope that others might be healed as well; or in the very least find some light even within the darkest depths.

IT's a sacred obligation to share - almost a sacred obsession.  It's just this side of being a sacred addiction.  

I'm not going all Sylvia Plath on anyone here - I'm doing what I am led to do - get it out and see what sort of sense it makes and then share it.   I know too, that I'm searching for some sort of balance in my everyday life - so it may appear disconserting to those around in my immediate space.  It may even look like I don't give a shit about 'normal', and frankly, I may not give a shit about it.

This writing does NOT give me an escape route - this is not an situation that allows me to hide from anything.  In fact, it is the opposite.  I'm facing head on things that have mired me down and kept me stuck for 43 years.
