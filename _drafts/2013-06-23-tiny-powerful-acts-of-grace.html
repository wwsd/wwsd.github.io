--- layout: post title: 'Tiny, Powerful Acts of Grace ' date: '2013-06-23T16:54:00.000-07:00' author: Jennifer Rowan tags: modified_time: '2013-06-23T16:54:17.044-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-741389610085892054.post-7027996991594266274 blogger_orig_url: http://splashofamermaid.blogspot.com/2013/06/tiny-powerful-acts-of-grace.html --- My mother - a beautiful, wise soul taught mys siters and I about grace and compassion in a tiny way that often returns to me.  If we spilled our drink - milk,  or water - even red cool aide - she would thank us.  She would say that that part of the carpet or the table or the wall needed cleaning.

She took that whole no crying over spilled mil thing and turned it into a lesson of grace.
