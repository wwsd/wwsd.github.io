I've got a few crochet prayer shawls I'm finishing up and I love to watch movies or something that filles my spirit when I create.  I recently asked my pals new and long-time for some recommendations.  Wow - they did not disappoint.

"Intouchable" - a French film that exhuded so much life I had to take breaks a) because I was laughing, crying or laughing AND crying so hard or b) becasue I simply never wanted it to end.  This movie captures the amount of extraordinary joy that can be found in the joy we see our beloveds - those we love experience....magnificent.  It's subtitled - but you'll forget that about 3 seconds in.
