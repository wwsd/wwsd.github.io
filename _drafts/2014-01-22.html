--- layout: post title: date: '2014-01-22T10:35:00.001-08:00' author: Jennifer Rowan tags: modified_time: '2014-01-22T10:35:30.213-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-741389610085892054.post-7796363689553456180 ---  I wanted to talk to you about this poster - it's such a touchy subject - I thought maybe we could chat about it offline a bit - I have a hard time with this one.  I've worked with dozens of breed rescue organizations that are indeed breeders - responsible ones, and who have taken in some dogs that have very breed specific issues, worked with them and rehomed them.  And the American Kennel Club helped me help THOUSANDS of animals after many devastating hurricanes.  I think we (as in the animal care world 'we") need to be careful to not generalize like this and sure, there are so many irresponsible breeders out there - but certainly not all of them.  I'd hate to see us lose the support that IS there.

It's a tough conversation to have - but I can tell you in the past week that two rescue organizations - one that I worked with in the past and the other I'm unsure of the name - that have had to close down for hoarding situations.
