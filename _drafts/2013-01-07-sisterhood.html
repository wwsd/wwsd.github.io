--- layout: post title: Sisterhood date: '2013-01-07T16:49:00.001-08:00' author: Jennifer Rowan tags: modified_time: '2013-01-07T16:49:51.923-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-741389610085892054.post-4800913399427661809 blogger_orig_url: http://splashofamermaid.blogspot.com/2013/01/sisterhood.html --- One of my sisters from my blood family left the United States again today to return to her home in Aisia.  She can't access a lot of social networking sites there, and it's been such a joy to see her take on things for nearly a month - her photos, her words - her joy and even her sadness.  Not to mention a phone call on Christmas that left us both dying -nearly literally - from laughter. 

Another sister of mine is this kind of amazing as well.  She and I have spoken on the phone nearly every single day for gosh - over three years now.  We have coffee togehter, we dream, we cry, we mourn, we share and we learn.
