All my life Sunday has been a sacred, recharging, special day for me.  Trust me, it wasn't because I went to church, there wasn't ever an repentence.  What there was is this space of serenity, love and happy recharge unlike any other day. 

Last weekend a dear pal was here and went to the store - to get makings for pot roast and gravy and pototatoes. 

He pulled into the drive with this I just swalled the canary look and what to my wondering eyes shoudl appear but sunflowers and Irish butter (complete, I might add) with a yellow ribbon AND a cheesecake that someone had graciously decorated with the words "Happy Sunday".

The tales of his advntures at the grocery store that particular trip are far too numberous to list here.  Suffice to say by the time he left - he'd made friends with half the staff and several other fellow consumers.

His only regret was that I wasn't there to see all that chaotic joy that he created.  Because, as my pal, he knows there is no finer gift to me that observering and or creating joy.  (it even tops crochet and that is VERY high on my joy meter scale)

We laughed for hours over the entire experience - I might add, his gravy was AMAZING on top of the mashed potatoes and for a girl who loves broccoli as much as she loves to breath, I never even got there.

Part of what we laughed (joyfully, fyi) about was the fact that it is indeed possible to share joy and create ripple effects.  The checkout person looked at the cake and said "I want a happy Sunday cake" and Tall,(read: coudl have just retired from the NFL) well built, plthe man soudns like super manly man that put the yellow bows on the Irish butter was all in too.

I mean how many times do youever in your life wrap irish butter with a curly ribbon anyway 

So, I think what this boils down ti, is that we need to start a Happy sundy revolution.  There is a Sunday each week - and if even 4 or 5 of us startted to fun about in full on joy mode - well, I think there would be a lot more Happy Sunday cakes, and there might be a bit of a run on curly ribbin as well.

So, who is in?
