I'm beginning to come to terms with the fact that I'm a messenger; a kind of a conduit from the divine to others. 

I can often look at a person and see directly into the root of their pain.  Before you go all calling me a spiritual dentist, let's talk about it.

Do you want to know - do you REALLY want to know what is being shared with me to say to you? 

Let's discuss things from my perspective a bit.....I've been working on my intutive abilities for a few years now.  At first I studied it so that I could better communicate with the voiceless.  IN the midst of that learning process I realized I was actually learning to communicate for the sake of healing for all living creatures - myself included. 

And finally, it has dawned on me, that I'm just here to communicate.  To discuss.  To start dialogues.  To make us question.  To help us wonder.  To lend support.  To love.

Just as much as it is a challenge for you to understand what is happening with you - it's as diffuclut as it is for me to deliver a message you don't want to hear.

And if you are already set on your path - you've already made your choice, please do not involve me.  And please do not think you are a victim.  Or not forewarned, because you were, you asked for it you got it.  Toyata.

I think it boils down to this - until we are completely open to remaining fluid about everything, why are we doing it?

Avast ye matey's.
